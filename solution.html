Solutions of
Cloud Computing

Cloud computing is like renting computer power and storage from a big company that has a lot of them. Instead of having your own computer and storage at home or in your office, you use the company's computers over the internet.

Cost Savings: Cloud computing can save you money because you don't have to buy and maintain your own computer equipment. You just pay for what you use.

Flexibility: You can easily scale up or down. If you need more computer power, you can get it instantly. If you don't need as much, you can reduce it.

And the remaining solutions of cloud computing are the Accessibility, Security, Collaboration, Automatic Updates, Disaster Recovery and Green Computing.
